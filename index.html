About Maine Amphibian Guide

This interactive guide helps you identify and learn about amphibians found in Maine during "Big Night" migrations and throughout the year.

"Big Night" refers to the first warm, rainy night of spring when amphibians migrate en masse to breeding pools.

Use the flip cards to learn identifying characteristics, size information, and interesting facts about each species. Search or filter to find specific amphibians quickly.

Photos and information collected by local naturalists and conservation organizations.
